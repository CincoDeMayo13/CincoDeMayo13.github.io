What are the benefits?


There are a handful of benefits that mobile command centers ( MOBILECC ) provide to those who choose to use them. These include increases in productivity and opportunities that your business typically wouldn’t have access to. Some of them are as follows.

Decreased Response Time


Perhaps the most important benefit that mobile command centers provide is decreased response time.

This is especially true for law enforcement and emergency services. For example, MOBILECC can treat people in the affected area during a natural disaster as opposed to transporting them to a facility. A mobile command center will also have everything that workers need in order to do their jobs as efficiently as possible.

In scenarios like this, minutes can often mean the difference between proper treatment and tragedy. So, every second will count.

This isn’t limited to emergencies or crime, though. The more time you save for your business, the more opportunity you’ll have to generate revenue. For companies that could service a large handful of clients in a day with a mobile command center, it’s often worth the investment due to the utility it provides.

Flexibility


There’s only so much equipment that can be taken out into the field.
Surveillance operations, for example, often require an extensive amount of equipment and technology to perform correctly. And, this equipment may need to stay in this location for days (or even weeks) at a time. This is especially true for areas that have a high crime rate or locations that may be targets of terrorist attacks.

A MOBILECC provides the flexibility to bring the full force of your facility to a separate location, allowing you to engage in operations that would otherwise be impractical or even impossible.

You could also operate in geographical regions that would otherwise be difficult to work in or access , such as low-income neighborhoods or rural areas.

Protection For Your Equipment


Since working in the field often requires expensive equipment, you are often limited to what you can bring with you without a mobile command center. For example, factors like weather, size/weight of equipment , and the need for power sources are common obstacles that businesses will face. However, with a MOBILECC, it will serve both as a shelter and a hub for equipment to operate.

In addition , your MOBILECC could even function as a remote office, eliminating the need for you to be outdoors in the first place.

Its spaciousness will also allow you to keep everything you need in a central location as opposed to have multiple forms of transportation for your equipment. Protecting your equipment during use is also an added benefit, as there are many factors that could result in equipment being damaged (or even stolen).

Presence At The Scene


Remote operations aren’t always meant to be hidden. Event security, for instance, can highly benefit from having a mobile command center at the scene.

While having a mobile command centre will provide significant convenience for your operations, its presence alone will, in addition , deter would-be offenders. Preventing crime in the first place will always do more for security than an efficient emergency response. In the above scenario, a single MOBILECC could provide the following utility:

  • Serve as a mobile crime lab for investigations at the scene

  • Surveilling an area with thousands of people

  • Become a hub to provide fast, clear communication with other units

  • Serve as a mobile medical treatment facility

So, take this into consideration if your business is tasked with any of these responsibilities.

Customization


Since a vehicle used as an MOBILECC is meant to serve as a center of operations, you can customize it in ways that serve you best. This typically means installing equipment and removing vehicle components, but your options don’t stop there.

An issue with customization, though, is the extra time and cost requirements that it demands. So, it’s not uncommon for businesses to rent MobileCC instead especially when one is needed for more prominent operations, or is required for a handful of times each year.

This is especially true if an MOBILECC would only feasibly be required a handful of times each year.

Hence , your mobile command center can be ordered and customized on your behalf, allowing you to be more efficient and avoid spending much time getting it ready for the field. Be sure you don’t get more than you need, though, as the price of extra space or amenities that you don’t take advantage of can quickly add up.
